# User_Performance_Funnel
End-to-end funnel analysis project using Power BI to identify conversion bottlenecks, optimize marketing channels, and quantify revenue growth opportunities through customer journey analytics.

# Key Business Opportunities Identified

## 1. Regional Funnel Optimization

**Achievement**

Developed a funnel optimization strategy targeting an estimated **8.5% uplift in purchases** through regional drop-off reduction and conversion-rate improvement.

### Supporting Analysis

* The North region recorded a **91.3% drop-off rate on Thursday**, indicating a significant funnel bottleneck.
* Targeted marketing campaigns, particularly through **Social Media (13.13% conversion rate)**, combined with deeper analysis of regional customer behavior, may help reduce abandonment and improve Thursday's conversion performance.
* Aligning Thursday's conversion rate with Monday's benchmark conversion rate of **11.95%** could support an estimated **8.5% increase in purchases**.

### Recommendation

Implement targeted regional marketing initiatives and investigate customer behavior patterns within the North region to reduce abandonment and improve funnel progression.

---

## 2. Checkout Conversion Recovery

**Achievement**

Developed a checkout optimization strategy focused on reducing funnel leakage by improving payment accessibility and lowering shipping-related purchase barriers.

### Supporting Analysis

* The largest drop-off within the customer journey occurred between the **Checkout** and **Purchase** stages.
* Since users reaching checkout have already demonstrated strong purchase intent, abandonment at this stage represents a significant lost-revenue opportunity.
* Potential friction points include:

  * High shipping costs
  * Limited payment options
  * Payment gateway failures
  * Checkout-stage usability issues

### Recommendation

Improve payment accessibility through additional payment methods, enhance payment gateway reliability, and optimize shipping costs through strategic logistics partnerships to improve checkout completion rates.

---

## 3. Cross-Channel Marketing Optimization

**Achievement**

Quantified a potential **2.5% uplift in purchases** and **1% increase in revenue** through cross-channel conversion optimization.

### Supporting Analysis

* Google Ads generated the highest customer acquisition volume, while Email achieved the strongest conversion performance.
* This suggests an opportunity to leverage Google Ads for customer acquisition and Email for lead nurturing and conversion-focused engagement.

### Opportunity Assessment

* Benchmarking Google Ads traffic against Email's conversion performance indicates that approximately **16 additional customers** could be converted into purchasing users.
* This would increase the purchasing customer base from **1,080 to approximately 1,096 customers**, representing an estimated **2.5% uplift in purchases**.
* Average revenue per purchasing customer was calculated by dividing total revenue by total purchasing customers, resulting in a value of **118**.
* Applying this value to the projected purchasing customer base results in an estimated revenue of approximately **1.3M**, representing a **1% increase in total revenue**.
* This analysis demonstrates that measurable revenue growth may be achieved through conversion optimization of existing traffic without increasing customer acquisition expenditure.

### Recommendation

Implement a cross-channel marketing strategy that combines:

* **Google Ads** for customer acquisition
* **Email Campaigns** for lead nurturing and conversion optimization

This approach maximizes the value extracted from existing traffic while improving overall funnel efficiency.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis indicates that the largest growth opportunities are concentrated in three areas:

1. Reducing regional funnel leakage in high drop-off segments.
2. Recovering lost purchases at the checkout stage.
3. Improving the conversion efficiency of existing traffic through cross-channel optimization.

Collectively, these initiatives demonstrate how meaningful business impact can be achieved through data-driven decision-making, funnel optimization, and targeted marketing strategies without relying solely on increased customer acquisition spending.
